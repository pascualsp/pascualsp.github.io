About Me

Hello! The name's Serjohn.

I am a Software Engineer and Computer Science graduate from UC Irvine. During my time at UCI I have had varying experiences with Technology. I have done numerous course projects, the most significant of which was a full-stack movie e-commerce website built from the ground up. I was part of a program where I spent 10 weeks mentoring middle schoolers on the field of STEM as well as developing a mobile game with them. I was also a board member for an on-campus organization where my primary duties involved managing and developing the club website alongside performing leadership responsibilities. Recently I have been working as a Software Engineer doing Java-based Web Development. I have also been learning other Web Development stacks in my freetime. In particular I have been very interested in making Web Apps using the MERN stack. I am interested in all things technology and I enjoy the challenges and level of thinking involved in developing software.
