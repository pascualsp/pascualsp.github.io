About Me

Hello! The name's Serjohn.

I am a recent Computer Science graduate from UC Irvine. During my time at UCI I have had varying experiences when it comes to tech. I have done numerous course projects, the most significant of which was a full-stack movie e-commerce website built from the ground up. I was part of a program where I spent 10 weeks mentoring middle schoolers on the field of STEM as well as developing a mobile game with them. I was also a board member for an on-campus organization where my primary duties involved managing and developing the club website alongside performing leadership responsibilities. I am interested in all things technology and I enjoy the challenges and level of thinking involved in developing software. The notion of making something out of essentially nothing is what drives me to build software. If you have any questions or would like to learn more then feel free to contact me or check out my resume!
